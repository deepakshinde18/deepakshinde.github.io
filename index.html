About Me

I've been building for the web since 2012.

I have 5 years of experience creating beautiful web sites and APIs with Ruby on Rails, Resque, Rspec, React, AngulerJS and front-end technologies such as SASS and Compass, CoffeeScript, and responsive design.

  • I believe every problem is unique
  • I pursue the simplest possible solutions
  • I prefer to create lots of little things rather than one big thing
  • I don't compromise the user experience because of technical concerns

I specialise in full-stack (front- and back-end) Web application development using Ruby and Rails.

I take ownership of problems, from the initial conception stage through to delivery and iteration.

My approach is creative and user-oriented; measuring success by solving problems. I distill often-complex user and business requirements down to the simplest feasible solution, producing elegant user interfaces and experiences which respond quickly and predictively.

I work iteratively; delivering discrete functionality one piece at a time, providing users with value as early as possible.
